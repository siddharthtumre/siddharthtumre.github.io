Short Bio

Hello, I am Siddharth Tumre.

I am a Researcher at TCS Research, associated with Data and Decision Sciences (DDS) Research Area. My work revolves around the intersection of natural language processing (NLP) and software engineering. I develop deep neural models and optimize pretrained Large Language Models (LLMs) for tasks such as classification, Named Entity Recognition (NER), and Question Answering (Q&A), integrating them seamlessly into larger software systems. I am currently interested in information extraction, knowledge graph (KG) construction, reasoning, and factual generation from data using LLMs in multimodal settings.
